Services

We focus on creating custom AI tools for you. We meet to determine your use case, build the tool, monitor its behavior and track KPIs until it performs as expected, then release it fully to you.

Discovery & Use Case

We meet with you to understand how you work, where time or quality is lost, and what a successful outcome looks like. From that we define a clear use case and success criteria for your custom tool.

  • Stakeholder and workflow interviews
  • Use case definition and scope
  • Success criteria and KPIs

Custom Tool Build

We design and build a custom AI tool for your use case—aligned with your data, systems, and workflows. The tool is built to be secure, maintainable, and ready for you to run once we hand it over.

  • Architecture and design
  • Development and integration
  • Testing and validation

Monitoring, Metrics & Handoff

We monitor the tool’s behavior and track KPIs to ensure it performs as expected. When it’s stable and meets the agreed criteria, we release the tool completely to you—documentation, access, and ownership—so you run it going forward.

  • Behavior monitoring and KPI tracking
  • Tuning and validation against success criteria
  • Full handoff: documentation and transfer of ownership
