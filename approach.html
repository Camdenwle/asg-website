Approach

We meet with you, determine your use case, build your custom AI tool, monitor behavior and track KPIs until it performs as expected, then release the tool completely to you.

Phase 1 – Meet & Determine Use Case

We meet with you to understand your workflows, pain points, and goals. We define the use case for your custom AI tool and agree on success criteria and KPIs so we know when the tool is ready to hand over.

Phase 2 – Build Your Custom AI Tool

We design and build the custom AI tool for your use case—aligned with your data, systems, and how your team works. The tool is built to be secure, integrated, and ready for monitoring and handoff.

Phase 3 – Monitor Behavior & Track Metrics (KPIs)

We monitor the tool’s behavior and track KPIs to ensure it behaves as expected. We tune and validate until it meets the agreed success criteria. This phase continues until you and we are satisfied the tool is performing correctly.

Phase 4 – Release the Tool to You

When the tool is stable and performing as expected, we release it completely to you. You receive full ownership, documentation, and access. You run and operate the tool going forward; we are not in the loop unless you ask us to be.
