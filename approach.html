Approach

Aspen Strategy Group follows a clear, phased approach that starts with how work actually gets done today and ends with custom AI tools and workflows operating reliably in your organization.

Phase 1 – Diagnostic

Assessment of current infrastructure, data maturity, and readiness. We learn your operating model, map critical workflows, and establish a grounded view of constraints before proposing any AI-enabled tools or automation.

Phase 2 – Architecture Design

Definition of system structure, governance, and integration plan. We design AI system architectures that align with institutional standards and integrate with existing data, security, and operational workflows—so the custom tools and workflows we specify can be delivered reliably.

Phase 3 – Implementation Oversight

Structured coordination of deployment and risk mitigation. We support leadership teams and delivery partners with clear decision frameworks, progress visibility, and operational controls to move designed workflows and tools into production.

Phase 4 – Institutionalization

Ensuring adoption, performance tracking, and long-term sustainability. We focus on governance, metrics, and operating practices so that AI workflows and tools are adopted, monitored, and continuously improved—remaining aligned with organizational objectives over time.
