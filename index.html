What We Do

Discover Your Use Case

We meet with you to understand your workflows, pain points, and goals so we can define the right use case and success criteria for your custom tool.

Build Your Custom AI Tool

We design and build a custom AI tool for that use case—secure, integrated with your systems, and fit for how your team actually works.

Monitor, Measure & Hand Off

We monitor the tool’s behavior, track KPIs, and tune until it performs as expected—then we release it completely to you so you own and run it.

Who We Serve

Why ASG Strategy

We focus on building custom AI tools that solve your specific problem. Second to that, we monitor behavior and track metrics—KPIs—so the tool performs as expected before we release it fully to you. No long-term lock-in: once it’s stable and you’re satisfied, the tool is yours.

How We Engage

  1. Meet with you to understand your workflows and goals.
  2. Determine the use case and what success looks like.
  3. Build your custom AI tool for that use case.
  4. Monitor behavior and track metrics (KPIs) so the tool behaves as expected.
  5. Release the tool completely to you so you own and operate it.
